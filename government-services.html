Frequently Asked Questions

For KRA registration, you typically need your national ID or passport, a recent passport-sized photo, and proof of address. Our staff can guide you through the specific requirements based on your situation.

HELB application processing usually takes 30-45 days. However, this can vary depending on the volume of applications. We recommend applying early to ensure you receive your funds before the semester begins.

Yes, you can renew your driving license through eCitizen. The process is straightforward, and our staff can assist you with the online application, document upload, and payment process.

The processing time for a Good Conduct Certificate (Police Clearance Certificate) is typically 7-14 working days. However, this can vary depending on the current workload. We recommend applying well in advance if you need it for a specific deadline.
