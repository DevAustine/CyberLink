About CyberKenya

CyberKenya is a modern, fully remote cybercafe and digital service center designed to serve clients across all regions of Kenya — anytime, anywhere. Launched in 2018, CyberKenya was created to solve client issues exclusively online, offering a 24/7 "pocket cyber attendant" that eliminates the need for physical visits.

We do not handle hardcopy documents — all our services are conducted remotely, ensuring you get fast, efficient, and professional support without leaving your home, office, or wherever you are.
