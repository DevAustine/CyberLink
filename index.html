Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?
We offer a wide range of services including government document processing, academic assistance, job application help, and more. Check our Services section for a full list.
How can I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment by calling us, sending a WhatsApp message, or using our online booking system on the Contact page.
How long does it take to process government applications?
Processing times vary based on the type of application. Most services like KRA PIN retrieval or eCitizen account setups are completed within a few hours.
Do you offer online assistance?
Yes, we offer virtual assistance for various services. You can contact us via WhatsApp, email, or through our online service request form.
Can I get help with my tax returns?
Absolutely! Our team is experienced in filing individual and business tax returns through the iTax portal.
Do I need to visit your office to get assistance?
No, many of our services are available online. You can upload your documents through our website or send them via WhatsApp for quick processing.
What are your operating hours?
We are open from Monday to Saturday, 8 AM to 7 PM. However, online support is available 24/7 through our website and WhatsApp.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash payments. For online services, M-Pesa is preferred for faster transactions.
Do you assist with scholarship applications?
Yes, we assist with both local and international scholarship and university applications. We ensure your application meets all necessary requirements before submission.
