About CyberKenya

CyberKenya is a premier cybercafe and digital service center located in Nairobi. Since 2018, we've been providing top-quality digital services to individuals, students, professionals, and businesses.

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing affordable access to technology and digital services that empower Kenyans to participate in the digital economy.
